Welcome to the agricultural land management division

The Agricultural Land Management Division (ALMD) is an integral part of the Technical Directorate within the Ministry of Agriculture (MOA). In November 2013, the division was renamed from the Rural Physical Planning Division (RPPD). Its primary objective is to provide expert advisory services on soil and agricultural land use management....
